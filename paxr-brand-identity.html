The idea
A warranty isn't a document.
It's a promise.

When someone buys a warranty, they're not buying paperwork. They're buying the removal of a specific fear — "if this breaks, I'll be left dealing with it alone." Paxr exists to make that fear disappear. Not just when things break. Every day. Quietly. Automatically.

Peace
The emotional product. Not coverage. Not documents. The feeling that it's handled.
Agent
Paxr acts. It doesn't wait to be asked. It monitors, reminds, renews, and confirms.
Minimal
Every product interaction should take less than 20 seconds. Less friction is more peace.
Global
'Pax' means peace across every major language family. The brand travels without translation.

Symbol mark — explained

The almost-complete circle represents a warranty period — the arc of protection. The gap is the moment before Paxr. The jade dot closes it: handled, resolved, complete.

The symbol is used optionally alongside the wordmark. The wordmark stands alone in most contexts. Never use the symbol without the wordmark at launch stage.

02 · Colour
Five colours. One story.

Deep navy is the world before Paxr — the problem space. Jade is the resolution. Stone and mist are the surfaces everything lives on. This is the full palette.

Motion principles
ease-out cubic
All UI transitions. Fast start, gentle landing. The feeling of something settling into place — like a warranty being registered.
200ms standard
Default transition duration for interactive elements. Fast enough to feel responsive. Slow enough to feel considered.
jade pulse
The only decorative animation: a single jade pulse on the status dot when a warranty is confirmed. One beat. Not a loop.
no bounce
Paxr never bounces. Spring animations signal playfulness. Paxr is settled, certain, resolved. Ease only.
